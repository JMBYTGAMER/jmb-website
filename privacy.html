🔐 Security Shield - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Security Shield collects, uses, and protects data when you use the bot.

---

1. Information We Collect

To provide functionality, the bot may collect and process:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Message content (only for moderation purposes)
  • Settings configured by server administrators
---

2. How We Use Data

The collected data is used strictly to:

  • Provide moderation features (bad word filter, anti-spam, etc.)
  • Store server-specific settings
  • Improve bot performance and reliability
---

3. Data Storage

Data may be stored temporarily or persistently depending on the feature. We take reasonable steps to protect stored data from unauthorized access.

---

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is only accessed by the bot system for its intended functionality.

---

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request removal of your data
  • Stop using the bot at any time
  • Remove the bot from your server
---

6. Data Retention

Data is kept only as long as necessary for bot functionality. Removing the bot from a server may result in deletion of related data.

---

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

8. Children's Privacy

Security Shield is not intended for users under 13 years of age, in accordance with Discord Terms of Service.

---

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept the updated policy.

---

10. Contact

For questions or data requests, contact the developer via Discord.
